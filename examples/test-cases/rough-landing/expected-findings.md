# Expected Findings

## Critical

- Mobile risk: hero actions and mockup use fixed horizontal sizing and can overflow or become cramped at 375px.

## Major

- AI template smell: too many badges, metrics, cards, and vague slogans.
- Visual system inconsistency: pill buttons, large cards, small cards, and mockup panels use unrelated radii and borders.
- Missing interaction states: buttons and links do not define visible focus states.
- Content credibility risk: metrics and customer-like claims appear invented.

## Minor

- Background glow and grid effects are visually louder than the content.
- Typography relies on oversized headline weight instead of clear content.
